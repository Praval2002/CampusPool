What Is Campus Pool?


Travelling becomes difficult and expensive considering SNIOE's location . We all like going together. Campus Pool will help you to connect with other students who are travelling on the same route and same time. It helps commuters to start sharing the ride instead of travelling alone. Innovative technology to discover, coonect , route-match , coordinate and cost-share in seamless manner. It automates end to end process of carpooling and makes pooling safe , comfortable and affordable for the students of SNIOE.
